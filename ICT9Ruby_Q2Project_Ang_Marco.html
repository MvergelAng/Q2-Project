Love the Philippines

The Philippines, an archipelago in Southeast Asia, is a tropical paradise renowned for its stunning landscapes, diverse ecosystems, and warm hospitality. With over 7,000 islands, the country boasts an incredible array of natural wonders, making it a haven for travelers seeking breathtaking scenery and unique cultural experiences.

Siargao Siargao is a tear-drop shaped island in the Philippine Sea situated 196 kilometers southeast of Tacloban.

Western Visayas Western Visayas , officially designated as Region VI, is an administrative region in the Philippines occupying the western section of the Visayas.

Zamboanga

Zamboanga City, known for its old Spanish fort, Fort Pilar. A tourist destination besides being considered as Highly Urbanized and Independent city in the region.
